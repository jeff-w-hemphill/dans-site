About Us

Welcome to The West Side barber Luxe Studio. West side luxe barber studio was founded by two passionate barbers with one goal in mind: to bring upscale grooming to Gilroy — blending comfort, style, and precision in one clean, welcoming space. We believe that every client deserves more than just a haircut — they deserve a full experience.

From sharp fades and detailed beard work to classic cuts and modern styles, we offer consistent, high-quality service for all ages. Whether you're coming in for a routine cleanup or a special occasion, you’ll leave feeling confident, refreshed, and always luxe.

Step into our chairs, enjoy the atmosphere, and leave looking — and feeling — your best. The West Side Luxe barber Studio. Stay sharp. Stay luxe.
